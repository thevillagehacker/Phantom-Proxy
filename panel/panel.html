          HOW SCOPE WORKS
          
In scope — shown normally

          
Out of scope — dimmed (DIM mode) or hidden (HIDE mode)

          
Wildcard: *.example.com or api-*.cdn.com (* = any chars)

          
Path: example.com/api/* — match host + path

          
Regex: /pattern/i — full JS regex against host or URL
